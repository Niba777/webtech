Bonds

Bonds are fixed-income investments where investors lend money to an entity (government or corporation) for a defined period in exchange for periodic interest payments and the return of principal at maturity.

Why Invest in Bonds?

Bonds provide a stable and predictable income source, making them a preferred choice for risk-averse investors. They can help diversify a portfolio and act as a hedge against stock market volatility.

Types of Bonds

  • Government Bonds
  • Corporate Bonds
  • Municipal Bonds
  • High-Yield (Junk) Bonds
