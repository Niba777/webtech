Growth Stocks

Growth stocks are shares of companies that are expected to grow at a rate higher than the market average. These companies typically reinvest earnings into the business rather than paying dividends, focusing on expansion, innovation, and market dominance.

Why Invest in Growth Stocks?

Investors choose growth stocks for their potential to generate substantial returns over time. Companies in technology, healthcare, and emerging industries often fall into this category. While they offer high rewards, they can also be volatile and require a long-term investment horizon.

Key Characteristics

  • High revenue and earnings growth
  • Minimal or no dividend payments
  • Reinvestment into business expansion
  • Potential for long-term capital appreciation
