Introduction to Stocks

Stocks represent ownership in a company. When you purchase a stock, you own a portion of the company and may benefit from its profits and growth.

Why Invest in Stocks?

Investing in stocks allows you to grow your wealth over time. Stocks historically offer higher returns than other investment vehicles, though they also come with risks.

Types of Stocks

  • Common Stocks
  • Preferred Stocks
  • Dividend Stocks
  • Growth Stocks
