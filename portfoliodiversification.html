Portfolio Diversification

Portfolio diversification is an investment strategy that spreads investments across different asset classes to minimize risk and maximize returns.

Why Diversify?

Diversification helps protect against market volatility by ensuring that all investments are not concentrated in one asset type. It reduces the impact of poor performance in one sector.

Key Diversification Strategies

  • Investing in Multiple Asset Classes
  • Geographical Diversification
  • Industry Diversification
  • Risk-Based Allocation
